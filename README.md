# worldcup-moment-marketing
Turn each day's match into on-brand content for **any business** — in about one minutes.

## What it does
Give it the day's match moment and your industry. It returns one ready-to-publish piece:
- a **short post / article** (LinkedIn, X, Instagram, Medium, newsletter), **or**
- a **short-video script** (TikTok / Reels / YouTube Shorts), **or**
- a **set of social captions**.

It pulls the hottest talking point, bridges it to your industry (cleverly, never forced), and lands on a clear call to action.

## Who it's for
Restaurants, gyms, real estate, e-commerce, coaches, local shops, agencies — and any solo marketer or social-media manager who wants to ride the World Cup without hiring a copywriter.

## What's inside
- `SKILL.md` — the engine. Paste it into any AI (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini…), or use it as a `SKILL.md` in Claude / Codex / Cursor.
- `HOW-TO-USE.md` — a 2-minute setup guide.

## Quick start
1. Open your AI, paste in `SKILL.md`.
2. Tell it your **industry**, **brand**, and **goal**.
3. Each match day, give it the moment → get the piece → publish.

## Safe by design
Real facts only (never invents scores) · no betting/gambling · won't exploit injuries or sensitive events · FTC-aware (disclose ads) · won't fake official FIFA/World Cup affiliation.
