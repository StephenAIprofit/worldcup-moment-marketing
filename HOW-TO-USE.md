# How to Use — World Cup 2026 Moment-Marketing Engine

Turn each day's match into an on-brand post or short video for your business — in about two minutes. No writing skills needed.

---

## What you got
- `SKILL.md` — the engine (a single text file / prompt).
- This guide.

It works in **Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, Codex, Cursor**, or any custom GPT / agent builder.

---

## Step 1 — Load the skill (one time)
Open your AI assistant and start a new chat. Open `SKILL.md`, copy **everything** inside it, and paste it into the chat. Send it.

(That's it — the AI is now your moment-marketing copywriter.)

> Using Claude Code, Cursor, or a custom GPT? You can add `SKILL.md` as a skill / system prompt instead of pasting. Either way works.

---

## Step 2 — Tell it about your business (one time)
Send one short message with three things:

> **Industry:** [e.g., coffee shop]
> **Brand:** [e.g., Bean & Gone]
> **Goal:** [awareness / leads / promotion / foot traffic]

---

## Step 3 — Each match day, give it the moment
Send the **real** thing that happened in a match, and say what you want:

> Today's moment: [e.g., a 90th-minute winner sealed a 2–1 comeback].
> Give me: [an Instagram caption] / [a short-video script] / [a LinkedIn post].

The engine will:
1. Pick the hottest talking point,
2. Offer you 2–3 angles that tie it to your business,
3. Write the finished piece for the angle you choose.

---

## Step 4 — Review & publish (30 seconds)
- Check the facts are right (it won't invent scores, but always glance).
- Tweak the wording to sound like you.
- Add your real offer / link if you have one.
- Post.

---

## Quick example
**You send:**
> Industry: coffee shop. Brand: Bean & Gone. Goal: foot traffic.
> Today's moment: a 90th-minute winner sealed a dramatic 2–1 comeback. Give me a short Instagram caption.

**You get back (sample):**
> Down a goal, then a 90th-minute winner. Comebacks taste better with caffeine. ☕
> Whatever the score tonight, we open early and pour strong. First 20 fans in the jersey get a free shot of espresso.
> — Bean & Gone
> *(Watch responsibly.)*

Pick the angle you like, post it, done.

---

## Tips for the best results
- **Feed it real moments.** Give it the actual result or talking point — the more specific, the sharper the post.
- **Don't force it.** If an angle feels like a stretch, ask for a cleaner one. A weak tie-in is worse than skipping a day.
- **Add your real offer.** The engine won't invent prices or promos — tell it yours and it'll weave them in.
- **One match a day is plenty.** Pick the biggest talking point; you don't need to cover every game.

---

## Good to know (built-in guardrails)
- Real facts only — it will never make up scores, players, or events.
- No betting or gambling content.
- It won't fake official FIFA / World Cup affiliation or use protected logos — keep your posts referring to "last night's game" generically.
- If you run ads or partnerships, disclose them (e.g., #ad).

That's everything. Have fun riding the world's biggest event. ⚽
